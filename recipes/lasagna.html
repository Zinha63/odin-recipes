Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layer pasta dish typically consisting of meat, cheese and noodles

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles

  • Ground Meat

  • Cheese

  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil.

  2. Add noodles to water till tender.

  3. Cook meat in a pan until brown.

  4. Layer noodles with meat, cheese and tomato sauce.

  5. Place in over till cooked.
