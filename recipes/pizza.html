Pizza

Description

A pie with tomato sauce, cheese and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Dough

  • Sauce

  • Cheese

  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Strech dough into a circle.

  2. Spread on sauce evenly.

  3. Add toppings of your choice.

  4. Bake in oven till cheese is melted and dough is browned.

  5. Let sit then slice and enjoy.
